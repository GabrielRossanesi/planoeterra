# AGENTS.md

## General Behavior

This project prioritizes premium UI/UX, strong visual hierarchy, and clean composition.

Avoid generic layouts, excessive cards, and cluttered interfaces.

Always aim for a modern, high-end look inspired by Linear, Vercel, and premium SaaS products.

---

## Frontend Guidelines

For any landing page, visual section, or UI refinement:

- Use the `frontend-skill`
- Prioritize composition over components
- Use strong imagery (real-world visuals when possible)
- Keep copy short and impactful
- Maintain high contrast and readability

---

## Plano & Terra Specific Rules

- The site represents a professional topography/engineering service
- Visuals must convey trust, precision, and technical authority
- Prefer drone images, terrain visuals, maps, and real-world context
- Avoid generic icons or abstract visuals

---

## Conversion Focus

Even when using strong visual design:

- Always include a clear CTA (WhatsApp or contact)
- Keep services understandable
- Do not sacrifice clarity for aesthetics

---

## When NOT to use frontend-skill

- Forms
- Admin panels
- Data-heavy UI
- Backend logic

In these cases, prioritize clarity and usability over visual impact.
